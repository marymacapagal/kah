How to play this horrible game
  • The game starts with a Card Czar (chosen by the computar) displaying a Black Card to the other players.
  • You, the player, will choose a White Card from your hand to complete or answer the Black Card.
  • After everyone submits their White Card, the Card Czar chooses the funniest one.
  • The player whose answer gets chosen is awarded an Awesome Point.
  • Awesome Points are non-redeemable and aren't worth shit. Except for bragging that you have the most horrible sense of humor within your group of friends.
  • The next turn starts with another Card Czar and another Black Card.
  • The game ends when an especially witty and horrible person gets 4 Awesome Points.
