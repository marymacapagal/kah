The Game

  • KAH is played in rounds with at least 3 players.

  • Each round starts with a kdrama-related question or statement. Your goal is to answer or complete the questions using Answer Cards given to you randomly by the game.


  • Once everyone has submitted an Answer Card, the Card Mama chooses the best one, awarding that player an Awesome Point.


  • When everyone is ready, a new round will start. The game will pick another player to act as Card Mama while the rest get a new Answer card to replace the one used in the previous round.

  • The game ends when someone collects 4 Awesome Points.
